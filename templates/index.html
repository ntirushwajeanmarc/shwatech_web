Arduino

"Arduino is an open-source electronics platform based on easy-to-use hardware and software." Arduino is the go-to platform for bringing your electronics projects to life. It's perfect for beginners and seasoned makers alike, offering a user-friendly environment for programming and connecting various sensors, actuators, and other components. With Arduino, you can unleash your creativity to build anything from simple LED blinkers to complex robotics projects. Its open-source nature and vast community support ensure that there's always someone around to help you troubleshoot or take your project to the next level.
